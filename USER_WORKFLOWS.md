# Clinic Management System - User Workflows

This document outlines the workflows and capabilities available to each user role within the Clinic Management System.

---

## 1. Patient Role
Patients interact primarily with the public-facing pages and their own dedicated portal.

### Core Workflows:
*   **Authentication:** Registration, Login, and Password Recovery.
*   **Dashboard:** View upcoming appointments and personal health summaries.
*   **Appointments Management:**
    *   Book new appointments.
    *   View upcoming and past appointments.
    *   Cancel pending appointments.
*   **Medical Records:** View personal medical history, past diagnoses, and treatment records.
*   **Profile Management:** Update personal details, contact information, and medical background.
*   **Information & Guides:** Access the patient guide, view clinic services, packages, and announcements.

---

## 2. Staff Role
Staff members (receptionists/nurses) handle patient reception, initial triage, scheduling, and clinic operations.

### Core Workflows:
*   **Check-In/Attendance:**
    *   Daily shift check-in (required before accessing modules).
    *   Clock-in/out and break management.
    *   Submit attendance correction requests.
*   **Patient Flow Dashboard:** Monitor real-time status of patients in the clinic and update flow statuses (e.g., Arrived, Vital Signs, Consulting, Pharmacy, Completed).
*   **Patient Registration & QR Scanning:**
    *   Register new walk-in patients.
    *   Scan patient QR codes for fast attendance confirmation.
*   **Appointments Management:**
    *   View all clinic appointments.
    *   Create appointments for walk-in or call-in patients.
    *   Update appointment statuses and manage invoicing.
*   **Doctor Schedule Management:** View doctor availability and manage daily rosters.
*   **Leave & Payslips:** Apply for staff leaves, view statuses, and access own payslips.
*   **To-Do List:** Manage daily operational tasks assigned to staff.
*   **Profile Management:** Update personal details and change passwords.

---

## 3. Doctor Role
Doctors are the core healthcare providers, managing patient consultations, medical records, and specialized clinic workflows.

### Core Workflows:
*   **Check-In/Attendance:**
    *   Daily shift check-in (required before accessing modules).
    *   Clock-in/out and break management.
*   **Waiting Patients:** Accept or reject patients assigned to their queue for consultation.
*   **Consultation & Records:**
    *   View patient medical history and previous records.
    *   Conduct consultations, update medical records, and prescribe treatments.
    *   **Record Approval:** Submits medical records for final doctor validation/approval.
*   **Appointments Management:** View and manage personal appointments; generate invoices post-consultation.
*   **Referral Letters:**
    *   Generate formal referral letters for patients (standalone or linked to appointments).
    *   Save as draft, edit, issue, and download printable PDFs.
*   **Schedule Management:** Configure availability, working hours, and session times.
*   **Leave & Payslips:** Apply for leaves and download personal payslips.
*   **To-Do List:** Manage clinical and administrative tasks.
*   **Profile Management:** Update personal details and change passwords.

---

## 4. Admin Role
Administrators have full system access to oversee operations, manage users, configure settings, and handle HR/Financial workflows.

### Core Workflows:
*   **System Dashboard:** High-level overview of clinic metrics, revenue, and active patients.
*   **User Management:** Create, read, update, delete, block, and restore all user accounts (Admins, Doctors, Staff, Patients).
*   **HR Management:**
    *   **Attendance:** Monitor all staff/doctor attendance, approve/reject correction requests.
    *   **Leaves:** Review and approve/reject leave applications.
    *   **Payroll:** Calculate monthly salaries and approve/mark payslips as paid.
*   **Clinic Operations & Settings:**
    *   Manage clinic services, packages, and promotional announcements.
    *   Manage public-facing website pages (visibility and order).
    *   Manage system-wide settings (clinic name, logo, contact info, operating hours).
*   **Clinical Oversight:**
    *   View and manage all appointments across the clinic.
    *   View all referral letters generated by doctors (read-only access with delete permission).
    *   Review system-generated reports.
*   **Content Management:** Manage team member profiles visually represented on the website.
*   **Admin Profile Management:** Update admin details (name, email, phone, position, bio) and change passwords.
*   **Data Management:** Export system data (CSV/JSON), manage soft-deleted records (trash), and perform hard deletions.
